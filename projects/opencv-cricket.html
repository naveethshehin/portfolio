Dial Table Automation System

  • Project: Video Link

The Dial Table machine automates the assembly of a product by combining a top and bottom part through a series of six sequential operations arranged in a circular indexing system. Each station on the dial table performs a specific function, with motion and logic controlled by a Siemens S7-1200 PLC and safety interlocks managed by a Banner XS26-2DE Safety PLC. Features
  • Designed a fully automated dial table assembly system using Siemens S7-1200 PLC, WinCC HMI, and Festo valve.
  • Programmed five stations: Load/Unload, Mating, Press, Inspection, and Label Application.
  • Integrated IO-Link sensors, Cool Muscle servo motors, and safety devices (e-stops, light curtains, interlocks).
  • Implemented system passed PHSR review and achieved >85% uptime on SAT.
  • Emphasized real-time diagnostics, data collection, and quality verification.
